BNHCA 2020 ENDORSEMENTS

In 2020, BNHCA hosted candidate forums for the City of Berkeley Council and Mayoral races.

After reviewing responses to questionnaires, forum questions, and the candidate's records, BNHCA has officially endorsed the following candidates. We believe these individuals will be the strongest advocates for meaningful, effective housing & climate action on Berkeley City Council.

  • District 3: Deborah Matthews
